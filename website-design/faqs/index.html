Frequently
Asked Questions.

How much time does building a website take?

The time highly depends on the requirement of the project. Some may take 15 days while some may even take more than 120 days, depending on the complexity of the website as well as the revisions needed by the client. We will keep you updated throughout the development process.

Will it be expensive to get a website designed?

The cost of the website depends on your requirement and the package you select. We provide different packages for different needs and even customized rates on request. All our packages are affordable and offer the best service in the industry.

Can I keep track of my website while it’s in progress?

You definitely can! As we work on your website, we provide you with a username and a password so that you can check its progress at each step of the way. We are always open to feedback and suggestions as we truly believe in satisfying our clients.

If I make changes to my site, will there be any additional cost?

Our designers will provide you with multiple themes from which you will be asked to select a specific one for your website. After that, we will make revisions according to your requirements while its under construction.

If I have an existing website that another web firm built, can you help me update it?

Of course! We will help revamp your website and give it a fresh new look as per your requirement and market trend.

When will I get the copyrights of my website?

Once we are done with the development of your website, we will deliver a complete website to you and transfer the copyrights.

Do you write content for the website?

Yes. Our resourceful team will write the content according to the theme of your website upon your request.

Will I have complete ownership of my website?

Definitely! We will provide you with full ownership once your website is complete. But if you decide to take your site to another hosting service, our team will assist you in doing that in the simplest and efficient way.

What are the benefits of having a website?

In today’s tech-driven world, customers tend to search for any service or product through different online platforms. Having a website helps you in supporting your business through its online presence and also establishes your brand’s identity.

What is web hosting?

Web hosting is where your unique domain name is located and where the website it hosted. It allows online access to your website 24/7 usually done on ASP or PHP.
